
# Campfire
Used for cooking "Quick Meals". Increases the temperature in a small area while burning.
## Variations
- Scrap Metal
- Stone
## Unlock
This item is unlocked at start of game
## Requirements
### Scrap Metal
- Scrap Metal - 10
- Sticks - 5
### Stone
- Stone - 10
- Sticks - 5
